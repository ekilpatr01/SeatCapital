The Opportunity

Private Markets
Deliver Superior
Returns

Private market investors historically have been rewarded with outperformance relative to public markets.  Further, private markets have offered a more diverse portfolio across sectors and stages of a company lifecycle, enabling the ability to spread risk and increase potential for strong returns. It is for these reasons, many of the world's most sophisticated institutional and private investors have maintained and in many cases increased allocations to private markets in their investment portfolios over the last several decades.

Seat Capital exists to give a select group of investors a permanent, well-resourced seat at that table. Our platform combines proprietary deal flow, deep manager relationships, and institutional-grade due diligence to deliver access that was previously reserved for the world's largest institutions.

"The private markets premium is not a myth — it is the reward for access, patience, and institutional process. Seat Capital delivers all three."
